Diphenhydramine is an antihistamine and sedative mainly used to treat allergies, insomnia, and symptoms of the common cold. 
It is also less commonly used for tremors in parkinsonism, and nausea. 
It is taken by mouth, injected into a vein, injected into a muscle, or applied to the skin.
Diphenhydramine can also be used to help you relax and fall asleep.
